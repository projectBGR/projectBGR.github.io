# Unlimited Power Notes

## The Ultimate Successful Formula
---
1. KNOW THE OUTCOME-precisely define what you want.
2. TAKE ACTION.
3. DEVELOP SENSORY ACUITY TO RECOGNIZE THE KINDS OF RESPONSES YOU'RE GETTING. (ARE YOU GETTING CLOSE TO YOUR OUTCOME OR FURTHER FROM IT?)
4. DEVELOP FLEXIBILITY TO CHANGE YOUR APPROACH IF YOU'RE NOT GETTING CLOSER TO YOUR OUTCOME.

**GIVE YOURSELF THE FIRE YOU NEED TO DO WHATEVER IT TAKES TO SUCCEED!** 

## 7 FUNDAMENTAL CHARACTER TRAITS OF SUCCESS
---
1. **PASSION**: 
- Gives life power, juice, and meaning. There is no greatness without a passion to be a great- parent, athlete, scientist, artist, etc...
2. **BELIEF**:
- passion and belief provide the fuel towards excellence.
3. **STRATEGY**: 
- a method of organizing resources that support your goals.
4. **CLARITY OF VALUES**:
- the fundamental ehtical, moral, and practical evaluations we make about what's most important in our lives.
- we don't just aim to achieve our goals but live a life style that aims to mirror the qualities required to achieve them.
5. **ENERGY**: 
- people of excellence have all the energy to take opportunities and shape them. 
6. **RAPPORT-BONDING POWER**:
- the ability to connect with people at the deepest levels of your heart.
7. **MASTERY OF COMMUNICATION**:
- the essence of what the book is about. People who fail take the adversities of life and accept them as limitations; People who succeed have learned to turn
those adversities into challenges that teach them strength. The people who shape our lives and our cultures also have the ability to communicate a vision or
a quest or a joy or a mission to others. Every chapter of this book deals in some way the mastery of internal and external communciation.

## CHAPTER 1
---
- The quality of life is the quality of our communication.
- Nothing has any meaning, except the meaning we give it.
- We produce two forms of communication:
  - INTERNAL: how we picture, say, and feel within ourselves.
  - EXTERNAL: words, tonalities, facial expressions, body posture, and physical action.
- Every communication we make is a cause set in motion (which in turn produces a result).
- We already produce results... but we need to produce good results NOT bad ones.
- COMMUNICATION IS POWER
- POWER IS THE ABILITY TO ACT
- Power needs to be shared with others. Not isolated to yourself.

### Exercises
1. When you are producing the result called "happiness":
  - What do you picture?
  - What do you say to yourself?
  - What do you feel in your body?
  - Do those thing now, and notice how easily you can produce that result by manipulating the way you communicate to yourself.
  - If you cannot think of what you see, say, etc., just act as if you can- ask yourself:
    - If I were picturing something, what would it be?
    - If I were to say something to myself, what would I be saying to myself?
  - Make a list of what you feel passionate about in your life?
  - What beliefs do you have about what you feel passionate about?
  - How do you communicate to yourself when you feel pasionate:
    - what do you picture?
    - what do you say to yourself?
    - what do you feel in your body when you feel passionate?

## CHAPTER 2
---
- Success leaves clues.
- **Modeling**: When one precisely reproduces the same specific mental and physical actions of another person, you will get same results.
- Ingredients for Modelling:
  1. Belief System: Whatever you believe you're capable of- you are right.
  2. Mental Syntax: Way people organize their thoughts. Mental syntax is like a code (ingredients and order).
  3. Physiology: Mind and body are totally linked. The way you use your physiology- how you breather and hold your body, posture, facial expressions, the nature and quality of your movements. These things determine the state you are in. **THE STATE YOU ARE IN THEN WILL DETERMINE THE RANGE AND QUALITY OF THE BEHAVIORS YOU ARE ABLE TO PRODUCE**.
